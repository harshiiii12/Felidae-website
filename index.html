About Our Company

Established in 2015, Felidae India Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a premier IT systems integrator and solutions provider, serving exclusively the Indian Government sector. Guided by the vision of our Director, Mr. Rajinder Kumar Sharma, we aim to empower ministries and government organizations with cutting-edge technology solutions tailored to their unique operational needs.
